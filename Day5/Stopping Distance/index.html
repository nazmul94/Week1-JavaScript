Welcome to Nazmul's Stopping Distance Calculator page!

This page attempts to raise awareness with regards to wreckless driving. The stopping distance is a measure of the distance covered by a vehicle when it attempts to come to a halt. This can be decomposed by the following equation:
stopping distance = thinking distance + braking distance

The Calculator

The speed at which a vehicle is travelling is, intuitively, directly proportional to the stopping distance. There are several other factors that can significantly affect a driver's stopping distance. A few are considered in this simplified calculator to provide an estimate of the stopping distance in diffent conditions.
